Custom Formulation

At Alluris Cosmetics, we understand that every brand is unique, and we're committed to helping you bring your vision to life. Our custom formulation services empower you to create cosmetics that not only reflect your brand's identity but also meet the specific needs and preferences of your target audience. Whether you're looking to develop a new product from scratch or improve an existing formula, our experienced team of chemists and researchers will work closely with you every step of the way.

With our personalized approach to formulation, you have the flexibility to choose the ingredients, textures, scents, and performance characteristics that align with your brand values and objectives. From skincare to color cosmetics, we leverage our expertise and industry insights to develop formulations that not only meet regulatory standards but also exceed customer expectations. Partnering with Alluris Cosmetics for custom formulation ensures that your products stand out in the market, offering innovation, quality, and authenticity that resonate with your audience.

The Custom Formulation Process


Consultation: Our process begins with a thorough consultation to understand your brand, target audience, and product goals. We'll collaborate closely with you to define the desired characteristics of your custom formulations, including ingredients, textures, scents, and packaging requirements.

Formulation Development: Our team of skilled chemists will then develop prototype formulations based on your specifications. We'll provide samples for your review and feedback, allowing for iterative refinement until we achieve the perfect formula.

Testing and Evaluation: Once the formulations meet your approval, we conduct comprehensive testing to ensure product stability, safety, and performance. We also welcome third-party testing and certification to further validate the quality and efficacy of your custom formulations.

Production and Scaling: Upon final approval, we initiate full-scale production of your custom formulations. Whether you require small batch production for limited edition releases or large-scale manufacturing for widespread distribution, we have the capabilities to meet your needs.
